🔍 現階段焦點：一場「防禦泥巴戰」🔍 Current Focus: A "Defensive Mud-Fight"

在反駁政府指控的「越獄（Jailbreak）」漏洞時，Anthropic 嘗試將 OpenAI 的 GPT-5.5 一起拖下水，主張這是前沿大型模型的行業共同技術邊界，而非單一企業的安全疏失。Anthropic 並指出，若以「發現單一狹窄越獄就須全球召回」為標準，將實質凍結所有前沿模型供應商的新模型發布。Rebutting the government's alleged "jailbreak" flaw, Anthropic tried to drag OpenAI's GPT-5.5 down with it, arguing this is a shared technical frontier of all large frontier models — not one company's lapse. It added that if "finding a single narrow jailbreak" triggered a global recall, that standard would effectively freeze new-model releases across every frontier provider.

而這場泥巴戰，已從法庭與輿論蔓延進每一位用戶的註冊流程。Anthropic 於 2026 年 6 月中旬更新隱私政策（2026 年 7 月 8 日生效，適用 Claude Free／Pro／Max 一般消費者帳戶），新增「驗證資料（Verification Data）」條款，官方原文為「為協助維持服務的安全無虞，我們可能會要求你確認年齡或身份」。依 Anthropic 官方身分驗證說明，這套機制委由第三方驗證商 Persona 執行，要求用戶出示政府核發實體證件並拍攝即時自拍（臉部比對／活體偵測），證件與自拍由 Persona 保管、不存於 Anthropic 系統，並於「存取特定功能、例行平台完整性查核或其他安全與合規措施」時觸發。放回出口管制的脈絡，這道「確認你是誰」的關卡，正是 Anthropic 為瓦解「無法在規模上驗證使用者國籍」死結、好讓頂尖模型重新對合規用戶開放的合規配套——代價，則是把實名與生物特徵驗證的門檻，潛在地推向每一個一般消費者用戶。And the mud-fight has now spread from the courts and the court of opinion into every user's sign-up flow. In mid-June 2026 Anthropic updated its Privacy Policy (effective July 8, 2026, for consumer accounts — Claude Free, Pro, and Max), adding a "Verification Data" clause; in its own words, "To help keep our services safe and secure, we may ask you to confirm your age or identity." Per Anthropic's official identity-verification docs, the mechanism is run by third-party verifier Persona — requiring a physical government-issued photo ID and a live selfie (facial match / liveness check), with the ID and selfie held by Persona rather than Anthropic, and triggered when "accessing certain capabilities, as part of routine platform integrity checks, or other safety and compliance measures." Read against the export controls, this "prove who you are" gate is the compliance scaffolding Anthropic needs to untie the knot of "no way to verify users' nationality at scale" and reopen its top models to vetted users — at the cost of potentially extending real-name and biometric verification to every ordinary consumer user.

Ultimately, as "Sovereign AI" — a strategic concept fusing politics and technology — becomes an extension of the national will, the nature of this conflict comes into focus: an irreconcilable collision between the state's "strategic concept" and "corporate self-determination."
